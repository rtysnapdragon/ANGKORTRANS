🌾 Modern Agriculture Business Flow – Cambodia
(End-to-End Operational & Digital Farming Model)
1. 🌱 Executive Overview

Modern Agriculture in Cambodia focuses on:

Smart farming (IoT + data-driven agriculture)
High-yield crop production
Supply chain optimization
Direct farmer-to-market integration
Sustainable farming practices
🎯 Goal

Increase farmer income, reduce waste, and connect agriculture directly to buyers using digital systems.

2. 🧑‍🌾 Key Stakeholders
Primary Users
Farmers (small & medium scale)
Agronomists / field officers
Buyers (wholesalers, supermarkets, exporters)
Logistics partners
System Operator
Agriculture platform company (your business)
3. 🧭 Core Business Flow (End-to-End)
STEP 1: Farmer Registration
Farmer signs up via mobile/web app
Profile includes:
Land size
Crop type
Location (GPS)
Farming history

✔ Output: Verified farmer profile

STEP 2: Farm Planning & Crop Recommendation

AI system suggests:

Best crops based on:
Soil type
Weather data
Market demand
Fertilizer schedule
Expected yield

✔ Output:

Crop plan per season
Cost estimate
Revenue projection
STEP 3: Smart Farming Execution

Farmers receive:

Daily/weekly instructions:
Irrigation timing
Fertilizer usage
Pest control

Optional IoT integration:

Soil moisture sensors
Weather alerts
Drone monitoring

✔ Output:

Optimized crop growth cycle
STEP 4: Monitoring & Data Collection

System tracks:

Crop growth stage
Health status
Risk alerts (disease/pests)

Field officers can:

Visit farms
Upload reports
Validate data

✔ Output:

Real-time farm dashboard
STEP 5: Harvest Prediction

AI predicts:

Harvest date
Expected yield
Quality grade

✔ Output:

Harvest schedule
Supply forecast
STEP 6: Market Matching System

Platform connects:

Farmers → Buyers

Matching based on:

Price
Quantity
Quality grade
Delivery time

Buyers include:

Supermarkets
Export companies
Local markets

✔ Output:

Pre-sale contracts (digital agreements)
STEP 7: Logistics & Delivery

System manages:

Pickup scheduling
Transport allocation
Cold chain (if needed)

✔ Output:

Delivered goods tracking system
STEP 8: Payment System

Flow:

Buyer confirms delivery
Payment released to platform
Platform deducts commission
Farmer receives payout

Payment methods:

Bank transfer
Mobile money
Agent cash-out

✔ Output:

Transparent financial flow
STEP 9: Feedback & Rating System

After transaction:

Buyer rates produce quality
Farmer rates buyer reliability
System adjusts future matching score

✔ Output:

Trust-based agriculture ecosystem

Seasonal flooding & drought patterns
Rice-heavy agriculture economy
Limited mechanization in rural areas
Mobile-first usage (low-end Android phones)
Khmer language support required
